About Me

I am Amteshwar Singh Khalsa, a student leader and youth representative currently serving as the President, District Jammu Urban – JKNC Students Union. I am dedicated to representing student voices, promoting youth empowerment, and working towards positive change within the student community and society. My journey in leadership began with a strong passion for student welfare, equal representation, and creating opportunities for young people to grow academically, socially, and professionally. Through active involvement in student politics, I strive to bridge the gap between students and leadership by addressing real issues, encouraging participation, and building unity among youth. Beyond politics, I also have a deep interest in technology, communication, and public engagement. I believe modern leadership is not only about raising voices but also about using innovation, awareness, and responsibility to create impact. As President of District Jammu Urban – JKNC Students Union, my focus is on strengthening student representation, supporting educational development, and inspiring young people to take active roles in leadership and community building. I strongly believe that today’s youth have the power to shape a better and more progressive future. This platform reflects my journey, vision, and commitment towards leadership, service, and empowering the next generation.

    Personal Interests

    Passionate about Gatka sport, youth empowerment, and community service. I enjoy outdoor activities and student engagement. Outside of my political and leadership responsibilities, I believe in living a balanced and meaningful life shaped by culture, discipline, and service. My personal interests reflect both my individuality and my commitment to community growth. One of my strongest passions is sports and traditional martial arts, particularly Gatka. Practicing Gatka has taught me discipline, focus, courage, and respect for cultural heritage. It is not just a sport for me but a way of life that strengthens my physical fitness, sharpens my mind, and instills values of self-defense and protection of others. Through this practice, I find fulfillment and a constant reminder of the importance of resilience and teamwork. I also enjoy spending time outdoors, as nature gives me peace, clarity, and a fresh perspective on leadership and problem-solving. These moments allow me to reflect on my goals and stay grounded, even while handling the responsibilities of student politics. Another interest close to my heart is youth empowerment and social issues. I am naturally drawn to working on matters like safety, equality, and opportunities for students—especially creating safe spaces for young people to express themselves and grow with confidence. This passion guides much of my leadership work and continues to inspire me in daily life. I value personal connections and meaningful conversations, whether with friends, peers, or community members. Engaging with people helps me understand diverse perspectives and strengthens my ability to serve others with empathy. Through all these interests, I aim to live with purpose, combining leadership, education, cultural pride, and community service. They are not just hobbies but guiding forces that shape who I am and how I contribute to society.
