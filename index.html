About Me

I am a student political leader and the Working President of NCSU at GDC Kunjwani, Jammu. I focus on student welfare, academic growth, and youth empowerment. I am Amteshwar Khalsa, a student political leader and the Working President of the National Conference Student Union (NCSU) at GDC Kunjwani, Jammu. As an active representative of the student community, I am dedicated to voicing the concerns, aspirations, and rights of young people within my institution and beyond. In my role as Working President, I focus on promoting student welfare, academic opportunities, and equal representation. I believe that students are not only the foundation of the educational system but also the future leaders of society. With this vision, I work to build an inclusive environment where every student feels empowered to express their ideas, contribute to decision-making, and participate in meaningful change. My leadership reflects my commitment to strengthening student unity, addressing challenges related to education, and ensuring that student voices are recognized in important discussions. My dedication extends beyond academic concerns to social awareness, youth empowerment, and community engagement. Through my active participation in NCSU, I strive to inspire my peers to take responsibility, engage in leadership, and work towards progress. My approach combines energy, responsibility, and vision, and I am proud to serve as a representative of GDC Kunjwani and a youth leader of Jammu.

I am naturally drawn to working on matters like safety, equality, and opportunities for students—especially creating safe spaces for young people to express themselves and grow with confidence. This passion guides much of my leadership work and continues to inspire me in daily life. I value personal connections and meaningful conversations, whether with friends, peers, or community members. Engaging with people helps me understand diverse perspectives and strengthens my ability to serve others with empathy. Through all these interests, I aim to live with purpose, combining leadership, education, cultural pride, and community service. They are not just hobbies but guiding forces that shape who I am and how I contribute to society.
